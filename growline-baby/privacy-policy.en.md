# Privacy Policy — GrowlineBaby

_Last updated: 7 June 2026_

GrowlineBaby ("the app") is built with privacy as a first principle. This page explains what data the app handles and how.

---

## 1. Data we collect

GrowlineBaby is designed to be **local-first**. Everything you create — baby profiles, names, birthdates, milestones, photos and notes — is stored **on your device** using Apple's SwiftData / SQLite framework.

We do **not** collect, upload or sell your personal data, your child's data, or your photos.

---

## 2. iCloud sync

If you enable "Sync with iCloud" from the in-app Settings screen, your data is replicated to your private iCloud account using Apple CloudKit. The data is end-to-end protected by Apple's iCloud infrastructure and is only accessible to you, on devices signed in with the same Apple ID. We never receive a copy.

---

## 3. Notifications

If you enable notifications, the app schedules **local** notifications on your device (reminders for milestones and moments). No notification content is sent through any third-party server.

---

## 4. Photos

The app uses Apple's PhotosPicker to let you attach photos to moments and milestones. Photos you select are copied into the app's local database. The app **does not access** your full photo library, **does not upload** photos anywhere, and asks for permission only when you tap to add a photo.

---

## 5. Firebase

The app integrates the Firebase iOS SDK for app configuration. We do **not** enable Firebase Analytics or Crashlytics personal data collection at this time. If this changes in a future release, this policy will be updated and you will be notified inside the app.

---

## 6. Children

GrowlineBaby is a tool for **parents and guardians** to record their own children's milestones. The app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect any data from children directly.

---

## 7. Your control

Because all data lives on your device:

- You can delete any baby, moment or milestone from inside the app, which permanently removes it.
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.
- If iCloud sync is enabled, deleting data on one device removes it from your other devices through CloudKit.
- The export feature lets you download a copy of your data (PDF baby book, image archive, or raw JSON bundle) at any time.

---

## 8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. The "Last updated" date above always reflects the most recent version.
