# Privacy Policy — HappyWay

_Last updated: 29 May 2026_

HappyWay ("the app", "we", "us") helps you build small, evidence-based habits and reflect on your well-being through weekly self-assessments. We care about your privacy and have designed the app to keep your personal data on your device wherever possible. This policy explains what data we handle, why, and the choices you have.

---

## 1. Summary

- Your habits, self-assessments and progress are stored **on your device**.
- We use **Firebase Analytics** and **Firebase Crashlytics** to understand how the app is used and to fix crashes.
- Signing in with **Google** is **optional** and only used if you choose to back up or sync your data.
- We do **not** sell your data and the app contains **no advertising**.

---

## 2. Data stored on your device

The content you create in HappyWay — your name, self-assessment answers, scores, selected focus areas, completed challenges, reminder preferences and the day chosen for your weekly self-assessment — is stored **locally on your device**.

You can erase this data at any time using **"Reset data"** in the app's Settings screen, or by uninstalling the app (if cloud sync is disabled).

---

## 3. Optional Google Sign-In and cloud sync

If you enable **synchronisation** in Settings, HappyWay can back up your data and keep it in sync across your devices using:

- **iCloud** on iOS, and/or
- **Google Drive / your Google account** on Android.

To do this on Android you may sign in with **Google Sign-In**. We only access the minimum profile information needed to identify your account and store your backup in your own cloud space. This data lives in **your** Apple or Google account, governed by their privacy and security policies — we do not keep a separate copy on our own servers. Sync is **off** unless you turn it on.

---

## 4. Analytics (Firebase Analytics)

We use **Google Firebase Analytics** to understand, in aggregate, how people use HappyWay (for example which screens are opened and how features are used) so we can improve the app. This may include a randomly generated installation identifier, device model, operating system version, language and approximate region.

This information is used for statistical purposes only. It is **not** used to identify you personally and is **not** sold or shared for advertising.

---

## 5. Crash reporting (Firebase Crashlytics)

We use **Google Firebase Crashlytics** to receive automatic reports when the app crashes. These reports contain technical diagnostic data such as the type of error, the device model and the operating system version, which helps us find and fix bugs. They are not used to identify you.

---

## 6. Notifications

If you allow notifications, HappyWay schedules **local** reminders on your device (your daily curiosity and your weekly self-assessment reminder). The content of these notifications is generated on your device and is **not** sent through any external server. You can turn reminders off at any time in the app or in your system settings.

---

## 7. What we do not do

- We do **not** show advertising.
- We do **not** sell or rent your personal data.
- We do **not** build advertising profiles about you.
- We do **not** collect the content of your self-assessments on our servers.

---

## 8. Third-party services

HappyWay relies on services provided by **Google (Firebase, Google Sign-In, Google Drive)** and **Apple (iCloud)**. When these services process data, they do so under their own privacy policies:

- Google: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- Apple: [https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

---

## 9. Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live (for example under the EU/EEA GDPR), you may have the right to access, correct, delete or export your personal data, and to object to certain processing. Because most of your data lives on your device, you are in direct control:

- **Delete** everything with "Reset data" in Settings.
- **Disable analytics-related processing** by limiting tracking in your device's system settings.
- **Turn off sync** to keep data on-device only.

For any request relating to data we process, contact us using the details below.

---

## 10. Children

HappyWay is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

---

## 11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. The "Last updated" date above always reflects the current version, and significant changes may also be announced inside the app.
